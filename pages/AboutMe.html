About Me

Hello, I'm Jake, a passionate software engineer on a journey to transition into the exciting world of game development.

My interest in game development started early in my life, and after exploring various career paths, I have come to realize that my true passion lies in creating immersive and engaging game experiences.

I have a background in software engineering with a focus on quality assurance, architectural design, and creating highly functional and maintainable code. My experience with build engineering, devops, and cloud engineering sets me apart as a well-rounded developer with a diverse skill set.

I thrive on challenging projects, and my fascination with mechanics and procedural generation has driven me to explore creating games with high replayability and emotional depth. I take pride in tackling complex problems and finding innovative solutions.

Currently, I am honing my game development skills with a particular focus on Unity and C#. I believe in continuous learning and have taken part in game jams to apply my knowledge and learn from others in the gaming community.

In the future, I aspire to create games that not only entertain but also leave a lasting impact on players. I am eager to work with experienced professionals and be a part of a collaborative team where I can contribute my expertise while learning from others.

My ultimate goal is to establish my own game development studio, where I can bring my creative vision to life and create games that inspire and resonate with players.

If you're looking for a passionate, confident, and adaptable game developer with a unique background, I would love to connect and explore opportunities to contribute to meaningful and innovative projects.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me. Let's create amazing games together!
